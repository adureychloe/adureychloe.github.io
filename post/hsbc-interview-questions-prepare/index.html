6、how to deal with conflict?

  • active listening. When conflict arises, it's important to listen actively to the other person's perspective without interruption or judgment. By truly understanding their point of view, we can begin to find common ground and work towards a resolution.
  • Finding common ground: Once I've listened to the other person's perspective, I try to find areas of agreement or common ground. This can help to deescalate the situation and focus on shared goals.
  • Collaborative problem-solving: Next, I work with the other person to come up with a solution that addresses both of our concerns. By working together, we can often find a solution that is mutually beneficial.
  • Following up: Finally, I follow up with the other person to ensure that the solution we agreed upon is working and that any remaining issues have been addressed.

13、how do you think AI affect banking?

  • Personalized customer experiences: AI can analyze customer data to offer personalized recommendations and services that meet their individual needs and preferences.
  • Enhanced fraud detection: AI can help detect and prevent fraud by analyzing patterns and anomalies in transaction data.
  • Improved risk management: AI can help banks analyze and manage risk by predicting potential problems and suggesting solutions.
  • Increased operational efficiency: AI can automate routine tasks, freeing up time for employees to focus on higher-value activities.
  • Streamlined regulatory compliance: AI can help banks meet regulatory requirements more efficiently and effectively by analyzing large amounts of data and flagging potential issues.

Overall, AI has the potential to transform the banking industry by improving customer experiences, reducing costs, and increasing efficiency. However, it's important for banks to balance the benefits of AI with the need to maintain customer privacy and security.
